  charter form remain open pending counsel. That is direct without being a decision.

  regulator mapping and names why the old text was wrong. Variant B withdraws the US framing and
  substitutes nothing beyond the settlement-agent fact. A reader choosing between them is choosing
  how much established context the baseline should carry, not choosing a legal position. Neither
  can be applied wrongly.

  satisfies the `CLAUDE.md` non-negotiable and does not quote or paraphrase SCC Law Art. 55.1 in any
  form. Deposit-insurance representation stays where it already lives: `01` §6.3 item 3 and `05`
