## Decisions

**D-name — error code is `429 LOOKUP_THROTTLED`, not the T1-drafted `429 LOOKUP_RATE_LIMITED`.**
Per the reviewer's naming note, I checked the actual precedent in `04_technical_architecture.md`:
- Per-row 429 codes use the `<X>_THROTTLED` shape: `429 REMINDER_THROTTLED` (04:822) and
  `429 REPORT_THROTTLED` (04:893).
- The only `RATE_LIMITED` token in the doc is the **bare, generic common code** `429 RATE_LIMITED` at
  04:751 — it carries no prefix and is the catch-all listed among common codes, not a per-endpoint code.
  There is **no prefixed `<X>_RATE_LIMITED` precedent anywhere** in the corpus.
Therefore the established per-row convention is `_THROTTLED`, and I used `429 LOOKUP_THROTTLED`. This is
the reviewer's recommended form and follows the precedent that actually exists.
